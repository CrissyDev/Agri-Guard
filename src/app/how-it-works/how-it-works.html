How It Works

A simple step-by-step flow to protect your rice fields — from sensors to results.

1

Deploy Sensors

Set up IoT sensors across your rice fields to continuously monitor environmental conditions and capture field imagery.

2

AI Analysis

Our AI engine analyzes images and sensor data every 2–3 days to detect weeds and pests at early stages.

3

Get Recommendations

Receive actionable treatment recommendations tailored to the threat level and your field conditions.

4

Apply Treatment

Apply treatments using drones, hand tools, or herbicides. Log results and track effectiveness.
